ABOUT ME

My name is Danny Meneses. I’m a junior at the University of Texas at Arlington. As of today I’m working towards a bachelor’s degree in computer science while completing minors in mathematics and french. Apart from getting my degree and minors, I have decided to dedicate my time in becoming a front-end web developer.

Recently I discovered my passion for web development. I get this sense that a website is an art piece were a person with a creative mind can express his or her creativity. Having a creative mind myself and experience in coding, what better way for me to express my creativity by creating and designing websites. All my websites will be impassionedly hand coded, for creating them I will be using latest technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and JQuery. Im still in the process of learning as I have just started but I intend to expand my knowledge and skills. This is my very first website and I intend to create more websites in the future and eventually become a freelancer.

Some personal facts about me, I am 20 years old. I was born in Los Angeles, California but raised in Arlington, Texas. In my spare time I'm usually drawing or coming up with new designs for websites. I often go to the movies, my favorite movie genre is drama/thrillers. I also have a big interest in photography, feel free to check out my gallery!
